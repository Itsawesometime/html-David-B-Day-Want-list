Who are the Wolfville Tritons?

The Wolfville Tritons Swim Club has been training competitive swimmers in Wolfville, Nova Scotia since 1972. We swim at the Acadia University pool, offering programs for all levels of swimmers ages 5 and up.

Tritons not only learn to swim, but they also build lifelong habits of physical activity and fitness, learn how to achieve personal goals, grow resilience and self-esteem, and work together in a team environment led by expert coaching.
