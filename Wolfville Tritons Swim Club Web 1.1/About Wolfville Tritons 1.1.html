Welcome to the Tritons

The Wolfville Tritons Swim Club is a non-profit organization that has been operating for over 50 years with a mission to teach children how to swim and help elite swimmers achieve athletic excellence.

The pool is a place for enjoyment, fitness and self-development. We are committed to providing a supportive environment for all of our members.
